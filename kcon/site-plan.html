Content

Home page

Step into the highlights of KCON 2025, where music, culture, and community all come together! Whether you were part of the crowd or watching from afar, this site provides a recap of the event’s biggest moments for you to enjoy.

#1 - Performances and Artists: KCON 2025 featured incredible artists that took over the stage and connected fans around the world. From experienced senior artists to rising stars, every performance represented K-pop’s unique sound and style. - Will include text and a button leading to a collection of highlighted artists who performed at KCON 2025 (organized by day) and a complete list of all the artists.

#2 - Event Information: Access information regarding the event’s details here. (includes text and button) Note: Make it detailed enough at the surface level of home page to know what it contains, but don’t provide the information itself at the home page level

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

The first child page will contain a collection of highlighted artists and performances (not all who performed or attended will be shown here, and the website will state as such). The collections will contain the group or artist’s logo when available. Below will lie a list of the remainder of the artists and performances that were not included in the highlighted section.

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

The second child page will hold the KCON 2025 event information. It will be an organized page with details of KCON including details such as: - What the location was - What the time was - Who the coordinators of the event were - What KCON as an event and culture means - What events made up KCON 2025 as a whole (this is optional, might decide to not do it) Brief history of KCON - These will be organized into clear visual sections that make it easy for the audience and viewer to easily find the information they might be looking for.
