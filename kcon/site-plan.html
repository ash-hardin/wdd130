Content

Home page

Step into the highlights of KCON 2025, where music, culture, and community all come together! Whether you were part of the crowd or watching from afar, this site provides a recap of the event’s biggest moments for you to enjoy.

Covering both incredible performances and unforgettable artists, these highlights will take you right back to the energy and excitement of the biggest K-pop event of the year.

Review information about the event, from details about the venue and coordinators to the meaning behind KCON.

Event Info

KCON began in 2012 as a small convention in Los Angeles designed to bring fans closer to Korean pop culture, music, and entertainment. Hosted by CJ ENM, it has since expanded into a huge global series of festivals featuring concerts, fan meet-and-greets, and pop-up events.
